Assassin's Creed II


Ваша подорож тільки починається продовження однієї з найвідоміших і найпопулярніших ігор історія ігрової індустрії. Довгоочікуване продовження набуло нового героя — молодого італійського дворянина Еціо Аудиторе та Фіренце, а також нову еру — епоху Відродження. В Assassin's Creed 2 не тільки зберігся основний геймплей, завдяки якому перша частина здобула оглушливий успіх, але й додалися деякі нові аспекти гри, які здивують та порадують будь-якого гравця.


Герої та злодії

Ezio Auditore da Firenze

A member of the House of Auditore, Ezio remained unaware of his Assassin heritage until the age of 17, when he witnessed the hanging of his father Giovanni and two brothers, Federico and Petruccio. Forced to flee his birthplace with his remaining family members—his mother Maria and sister Claudia—Ezio took refuge with his uncle Mario in the Tuscan town of Monteriggioni, at the Villa Auditore.

Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad

Tasked with the deaths of nine individuals who, unbeknownst to him, made up the ranks of the Templar Order in the Holy Land, Altaïr began a quest to change his ways and liberate the Kingdom from their corruption. During his quest, however, Altaïr learned of a plot far more sinister than he originally believed. In completing his mission, he also cleansed the Assassin Order of its treacherous leader, Al Mualim.

Amunet

Aya was initially a loyal retainer with high hopes for the young queen, playing a pivotal role in the Alexandrine Civil War, but in the aftermath of the Battle of the Nile, Cleopatra and her newfound Roman ally Julius Caesar sided with the Order and protected Lucius Septimius, one of their remaining members. Released from service yet feeling betrayed, Aya and Bayek founded the Hidden Ones to resume the fight against the Order, with the former resolving to establish a Roman branch at the expense of her marriage.

Juno

Juno also had a husband named Aita, whom she was forced to euthanize after an experiment went awry and left him catatonic, during their search for a solution to prevent the Great Catastrophe. However, she managed to immortalize him by altering the human genetic code to create continuous reincarnations of her husband known as Sages, who were humans born with Aita's memories.
